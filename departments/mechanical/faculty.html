Faculty and its people

Faculty Introduction:

The faculty is diverse in educational background, professional experience, and interests. The faculty are committed to teaching, research and public service. A number of faculty hold doctoral degrees in a variety of related fields. The faculty is enriched each year by new regular faculty appointees as well as return of faculty after completing higher education and research abroad. This infusion of new teaching and scholarly talents adds freshness and vitality to the Department of Mechanical Engineering.
