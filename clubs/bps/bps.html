Club Overview

BUET Photographic Society (BUETPS) is about the visual endeavours that the students from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) dare to procreate. Despite our inhuman academic schedules, we still strive to engage in creating works of art, of life, of revolution and what not. Our study of both basic and cutting edge (!) engineering haven't turned us into metal-monsters, orthographic bodies propelled by logical operations only, at least not yet. That is because we still keep ourself active in the process of "Creation & Composition", something that doesn't need to adhere to formatted rules and regulations. In that perspective " Photography " -is perhaps the best tool for countering our "Art Dyslexia" :) >> If you are new to BUET-PS, please introduce yourself in the Intro Thread
