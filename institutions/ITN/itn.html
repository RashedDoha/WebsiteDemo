Welcome to ITN-BUET

ITN-BUET is a network of knowledge and skill development in water supply and sanitation (WSS). It strives for expansion of knowledge base through networking for HRD that promotes sustainable development of WSS sector. You are invited to visit our site and learn about the Centre and its activities for the development of water supply and waste management.
